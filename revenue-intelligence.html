Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

Team Project (5 members)

Contributed to a multi-module Tableau dashboard analyzing ~$80M in revenue across product, geography, inventory, reseller channels, and sales performance to support business decision-making.

Scale~$80M revenue analyzed
FocusKPIs, segmentation, Pareto analysis
OutcomeClearer visibility into top drivers

Overview

  • Built a multi-tab dashboard system covering Revenue, Profit, Inventory, Resellers, and Salespersons
  • Included KPI tracking, time-series analysis, forecasting, and geographic views
  • Used Pareto analysis to identify high-impact products and revenue concentration
  • Analyzed product-level margins, reseller performance, and salesperson productivity
  • Enabled interactive filtering and drill-down exploration for decision-making

What I Did

  • Built KPI views for total revenue and performance tracking
  • Created time-series views to show revenue movement over time
  • Added geographic and product-level cuts for deeper analysis
  • Used Pareto and segmentation analysis to identify high-impact product lines
  • Designed interactive filters so business users could explore the data directly
